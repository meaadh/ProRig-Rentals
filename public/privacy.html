Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 10, 2025

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using our services

1. Introduction

At ProRigRentals, your privacy is important to us.

This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  1. 2.1 Information You Provide

    When you create an account or use our services, you provide us with personal information that includes:

    • Your name and contact information
    • Account credentials
    • Payment information when required
    • Communication preferences
    • Rental Details

  2. 2.2 Automatic Information

    We automatically collect and store certain information when you use our services:

    • Device information and identifiers
    • Log information and usage statistics
    • Location information when enabled
    • Browser type and settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize our services
  • Process transactions related to your rentals
  • Send notifications and updates about your rental
  • Maintain security and verify identity
  • Analyze and improve our services

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share personal information outside our company except in the cases below.

  1. 4.1 With Your Consent

    We may share information when you expressly consent.

  2. 4.2 For Legal Reasons

    We may disclose information when we believe, in good faith, it is necessary to:

    • Comply with law, regulation, or legal process
    • Enforce our Terms of Service
    • Detect or prevent fraud, security, or technical issues
    • Protect the rights, property, or safety of users

5. Data Security

We work to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

  • Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS) and SHA-256 hashing where applicable
  • Restricted employee access based on need-to-know
  • Periodic reviews of collection, storage, and processing practices
Important Notice

You are responsible for safeguarding your password and account activity.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to restrict or object to our processing of your information

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date at the top.

Your continued use of our services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of such changes.

By continuing to access or use our service after those revisions become effective, you agree to be bound by the revised terms of this policy.
